VENDOR SERVICE AGREEMENT
Provider: CloudVault Solutions

1. Services Provided
Provider will deliver cloud infrastructure and managed database services.
Provider will have access to customer's AWS S3 buckets, Kubernetes clusters, and CloudWatch logs for monitoring purposes.

2. Data Handling & Privacy
Provider will process Personally Identifiable Information (PII) on behalf of the customer.
Provider explicitly agrees that it will NOT process, store, or transmit any Financial Data or credit card information.

3. Compliance and Security Certifications
Provider maintains a SOC2 Type II certification, which is currently valid and renewed annually.
Provider is currently undergoing an ISO 27001 audit, but is not yet certified.

4. Service Level Agreement (SLA)
Provider guarantees a monthly uptime of 99.99%.
In the event of a security breach or unauthorized access, Provider shall notify Customer within 24 hours of discovery.
